Main Computer Case and Lacations

A computer case, also known as a computer chassis. Cases can come in various sizes.The size and shape of a computer case still determined by the structure of the motherboard since it is the biggest part of most computers.

The Motherboard The Power Support Units Drive Bay Buttons and LEDs Vents I/O Template The Picture of The Computer Case

-The motherboard:

Generally screwed to the case along its biggest face, which could be the base or the side of the case contingent upon the structure component and direction.

-The power support unit:

Mounting point differs from case to case, however the most usually utilized areas are at the back of the case.

(None)



-Drive bay

Most computer include drive bays on the front of the case.

-Buttons and LEDs:

Usually located on the frount of the case. Some computer cases include additional I/O ports, temperature, and processor speed monitor in the same area.

-Vents:

Are usually tracked down on the front, back, top, left side board, and in some cases on the right-side board of the computer case. No matter what their situation, their motivation is either to give cool air access to the case or to let hot air out.

-I/O template:

Fits around the motherboard's back panel of external ports.
